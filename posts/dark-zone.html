Dark Zone

Dark Zone is a small 2D prototype inspired by Among Us-style movement and visibility. The main goal of this first version is the dark vision system: you can only see what’s near your player.

Play the prototype

Play Dark Zone →

What’s in v1

  • Player: a white block
  • Movement: WASD / Arrow Keys
  • Map: 3 rooms + 1 corridor
  • Dark vision: only a circular area around you is visible

What’s next

Next I want to improve the map layout, add more game interactions, and eventually make the visibility react more to walls and rooms.
